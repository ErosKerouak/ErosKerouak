## Research

I am a geologist specializing in **geophysics and potential field methods**, currently pursuing a **Master's degree in geophysics**.

My research focuses on the **integration and processing of gravity and magnetic data** for geological and geophysical mapping, with an emphasis on **computational geoscience and reproducible scientific workflows**.

Main research themes:

- Gravity field modelling
- Bouguer and gravity disturbance analysis
- Crustal structure investigation
- Geophysical interpolation methods
- Equivalent sources modelling
- Geophysical data integration
